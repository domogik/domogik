=======
Helpers
=======

plcbus all
==========

To check if your device are correctly plugged or if your interface is able to see them, you may use this helper. This helper may take a few seconds (10) to give you the result.

.. code-block:: none

    $ plcbus all A 00
    A1 on
    A2 on
    A3 off
    A4 off
